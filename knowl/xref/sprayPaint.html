Exercise 10. Spray paint, Part I.

Suppose the area that can be painted using a single can of spray paint is slightly variable and follows a nearly normal distribution with a mean of 25 square feet and a standard deviation of 3 square feet. Suppose also that you buy three cans of spray paint.
  1. How much area would you expect to cover with these three cans of spray paint?
  2. What is the standard deviation of the area you expect to cover with these three cans of spray paint?
  3. The area you wanted to cover is 80 square feet. What is the probability that you will be able to cover this entire area with these three cans of spray paint?
